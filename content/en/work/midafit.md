MIDAFIT teaches fitness professionals. Courses, video lessons, membership.
Built from zero as a two-person company; the whole frontend is one person's
work.

The application runs on Nuxt with Supabase for authentication, data and
storage. Design system, video delivery, checkout — all in-house, no template.
